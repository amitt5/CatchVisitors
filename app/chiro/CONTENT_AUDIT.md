## 📝 Notes

- The new site actually has **enhanced** features compared to the original:
  - Interactive language toggle (EN/NL)
  - Clickable condition cards with detailed modals
  - Inline 3-step booking widget (more modern than external forms)
  - Voice AI assistant integration
  - Cleaner, more modern design

- The original site likely has more content depth (blog posts, newsletters, resource library) that accumulates over time. This is normal for an established practice vs. a new demo.

- Consider whether the demo is meant to showcase the **platform capabilities** (voice AI, booking, design) or to be a **complete replica** of the existing site. For a demo/pitch, the current implementation may be more than sufficient.
