## About Us

NAVANK was founded in 1998 by Mr Navin Singhal (now Chief Mentor of the company). In 2009, Naval Singhal joined the company as Chief Business Officer and filled up the massive gap in the quality and competitive raw material to produce Optical Fiber Cable in India and hence expanded the wings with the vision of making India most competitive in the World.

23 years down the road, NAVANK has become an integral part of Optical fiber, Data cable and Power cable market.

Driven by passion to help Customers, NAVANK has expanded into Freight Forwarding, Warehousing in India & USA by providing seamless Services from Manufacturer-to-Customer, so that Customer can focus on Manufacturing & we take care of all Logistics so that Customer can be more competitive in local & global market.

Today, NAVANK is one of the largest raw material Supplier for OFC, Data and power cable industries in India and slowly expanding into US and Europe markets.

### Key Focus Areas
- Sheathing Compounds for FOC & Power Cables (LSZH, XLPE, FR XLPE, HDPE)
- PBT Compounds
- Mica Tape
- ECCS and CJB Tapes for Cable Armouring
- Water Blocking Tapes and Yarns for FOC
- Freight Forwarding, Logistics and Warehousing

## Products — Optical Fiber Cable

### Water Blocking Tape
Water blocking tape typically used in power, fibre and copper telecom cable to prevent longitudinal water penetration. Available in Non-conductive, Semi-conductive and PET laminated versions to suit diverse cable applications. Available in JUMBO version & in various widths as per Customer demand.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Water-Blocking-Tape-500x400.jpg

### Water Swellable Yarn
Water blocking yarn with characteristics of quick water absorption, high expansivity, high tensile strength, good heat resistance, high chemical stability. Used in waterproof FOC & communication cable, dry-type cable, and insulation electronic cables.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/images-1-1-500x400.jpg

### ECCS Tape
The copolymer coated (ECCS) steel tape made from high quality cold rolled steel tape; copolymer film made from EAA material with excellent heat seal. Used for armouring the Fiber optical cable to protect it from rodents & external forces. Available in JUMBO version & in various Widths as per Customer demand.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/images-6-1-500x400.jpg

### CJB (Controlled Jacket Bond) ECCS Tape
Low carbon electrolytically chrome coated steel tape tempered to T3, suitable for telecommunication cables. Coated with a polyethylene copolymer film — exhibits adhesion to the steel substrate and provides a controlled jacket bond to materials LDPE, MDPE and HDPE. Available in JUMBO version & in various Widths as per Customer demand.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/images-8-1-500x400.jpg

### PBT Compounds for Loose Tube
PBT is one of the most popular extrudable polymer Compounds to make loose tubes as used in fiber-optic cables. Protects Optical Fibers by extruding a Loose Tube together with Optical Fibers (2-24 fibers) & Petroleum jelly, allowing the Optical Fibers to move freely inside the tube. Exhibits excellent extrusion properties at high speed with appropriate tensile properties meeting stringent Bending Test of FO Cables. Available in many colours along with suitable Master Batch.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/images-11-1-500x400.jpg

### LSZH Compounds
Polyolefin based Insulation & Sheathing Compounds available with Low Smoke Zero Halogen (LSZH), Halogen Free Flame Retardant (HFFR), Zero Halogen Flame Retardant (ZHFR) properties. Used for manufacture of Fiber-optic Cables, CAT-5/6/7 LAN Cables, Solar Cables, Automobile Cables, House wiring Cables, Railway cables, Elevator Cables etc.
Meets International Standards: UL1581, TUV, IEC60332, ISO/TS16919.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/500X500-12-500x400.jpg

## Products — Power Cable

### XLPE Compound
Cross-linked polyethylene used as insulation for all types of power and special cables in the wire cable industry.
- Peroxide-type XLPE available for all types of HV & EHV cable
- Silane-type moisture-cured XLPE available for all types of special cable and Sioplas lines

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/XLPE-cable-500x400.jpg

### Semiconductive Compound
Cross-linkable black polyethylene compound, specially designed for semiconductive conductor shields or bonded/strippable insulation shields of power cables. Semi-conductive layers (internal and external) designed to protect XLPE, EPR insulation layer.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Semiconductor-Shielding-Compound-500x400.jpg

### LSZH Compound (Power Cable)
Cable sheathing material widely used in the world. Provides better scale processing and better prices while meeting basic safety performance, flame retardant performance, mechanical strength, and reliability.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/LSZH-compound-500x400.jpg

### PE Compound
Used in wire and cable for sheathing and insulation purposes. All types available: HDPE, MDPE, LDPE & FR-HDPE for all types of cables.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/PE-compound-500x400.jpg

### Mica Tape
Insulation material made of superior phlogopite mica paper, synthetic mica paper, and calcined muscovite mica tape, impregnated with high-quality silicon resin and backed with reinforcing material. Resists high voltage and especially high temperature. Used in fire-resistant cables.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/images-10-2-500x400.jpg

### PVC Compound
Commonly used in low-voltage cables, telecommunication lines, and electrical wiring. Leading supplier of PVC compound for sheathing & insulation meeting all international standards.

**Image:** https://www.navank.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/PVC-compound-500x400.jpg
